PRAGATIv2.0: The story

It all started with the Kharagpur Data Science Hackathon 2025, where the Problem Statement asked us to create am agentic AI framework for deciphering the suitability of various research papers : IN JUST TEN DAYS. Now given some previous background of the team in building agentic AI frameworks, this wasn't too much of a steep challenge, but the tight timeframe definitely was. We uploaded our project to GitHub in the hopes of helping people looking for similar multi-agent architectures, but instead got rewarded by getting noticed by Ready Tensor, Inc. and getting an invitation via mail for their Agentic AI Showcase 2025. Well, we thought, if we're going to submit this to a showcase, why not prettify this a little and iron the creases in the application out? Midsems were a hurdle, as were the various inter-hall competitions happening in this timeframe (IITKGP is notorious for the hall tempo within its students), but as we have learnt during our stay in college, "if you want something really badly, you'll always find time for it". So here we present to you, PRAGATIv2.0.
